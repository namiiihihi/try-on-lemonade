**Shot 1 — Hook (0–3s)**
Nhin thang vao camera, bieu cam tu nhien. Co the dung text overlay de dat cau hoi: "Bao gio mua nham mau son lan cuoi?" hoac tu chon hook phu hop.

**Shot 2 — Mo app (3–8s)**
Quay man hinh hoac quay tay cam dien thoai vao lemonade.vn/try-on. The hien ro URL hoac nut "Thu mau".

**Shot 3 — Demo AI (8–20s)**
Quay close-up guong mat khi AI dang render mau son len moi. Show ro it nhat 2–3 mau khac nhau — vuot qua cac mau, chu y toc do chuyen mau phai ro rang.

**Shot 4 — Reaction (20–30s)**
Phan ung that — cuoi, ngac nhien, ung y mot mau nao do. Day la shot quan trong nhat de tao ket noi cam xuc.

**Shot 5 — Chup anh / chia se (30–40s)**
Nhan nut chup anh trong app, show ket qua. Co the cut sang anh selfie that de so sanh.

**Shot 6 — CTA (40–60s)**
Nhin thang camera, noi link: lemonade.vn/try-on. Co the cam san pham that neu da nhan duoc.
